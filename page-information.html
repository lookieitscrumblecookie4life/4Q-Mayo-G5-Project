CAN YOU BECOME THE MASTER BAKER?

Welcome to Good Pastry, Great Pastry — a sweet twist inspired by the charm of Good Pizza, Great Pizza! Step into the oven-warmed world of pastry puzzles, where each tile is as tempting as a cupcake and just as tricky as a perfectly folded croissant. Match delicious baked goods, rise through the ranks, and prove you've got what it takes to become the ultimate master baker!

Objective

Match all the bakery-themed tiles by flipping them over two at a time. Remember where each treat is hiding, and clear the board to win. It’s all about memory and strategy!
  1. Once you hit "Start," the timer begins ticking down!
  2. Tiles shuffle and flip face-down.
  3. Tap or click on two tiles to flip them over.
  4. If they match, they stay flipped over.
  5. If they don’t match, they’ll flip back face-down after a moment.
  6. Keep flipping and matching tiles as fast as you can.
  7. The game ends when all pairs are matched or when time runs out.
  8. Once all pairs are matched, you win!
